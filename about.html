About MbudStore

MbudStore was founded in 2019 in Cikarang by a group of four friends—two boys and two girls—who had shared a common hobby since childhood: collecting and studying shoe design (both sneakers and casual styles).

They often exchanged stories, photos, and shoe reviews on social media until they finally came up with an idea: "Why don't we create our own shoe store that reflects our love for shoes?"

Our Milestones

  • 2019 — MbudStore founded by folks who love sneakers.
  • 2020 — The official MbudStore grand launching.
  • Present — Doing our absolute best for our customers worldwide.

Our Vision

MbudStore's vision is to be a "home" for shoe lovers in Cikarang and the surrounding area who are looking for products ranging from casual to sporty styles, with unique yet comfortable designs.
